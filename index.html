About the harness

  • Weighs 140g / 4.9oz (under half the weight of others). 
  • Holds a 5–8L removable waterproof drybag.
  • Takes two minutes to install. No tools needed.
  • Pack your drybag off the bike, slide it into the harness and securely hold it in place with one buckle. The harness keeps your drybag protected and waggle free.
  • We recommend using the Ortlieb PS10 7L drybag. This is shown above. It’s surprisingly tough and weighs 54g, making the harness and bag less than 200g.

What the Wolftooth?

The Wolftooth Valais is an elegant product that fits on your seatpost and reduces your dropper travel. This helps avoid the bag buzzing the rear tyre.

If you do accidentally buzz the tyre the Louise harness protects your drybag, and the smooth plastic won’t beat your tyre up either.
